Welcome to MasonHub! This guide will help you get started with our comprehensive order management system, from initial setup to your first order.

## Quick Start Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Account Setup">
    Create your MasonHub account and configure basic settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Store">
    Integrate your e-commerce platform (Shopify, EDI, or API)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure SKUs">
    Set up your product catalog and inventory management
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Orders">
    Create test orders to verify your configuration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Start processing real orders through MasonHub
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Access

### Login Requirements

To access the MasonHub Order Management System, you'll need:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Account Name" icon="building">
    Your Shopify slug or store name as it appears in your URL
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Address" icon="envelope">
    Your registered email address
  </Card>
  <Card title="Password" icon="lock">
    Your secure account password
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  First-time users should check their email for account activation instructions after registration.
</Tip>

## Integration Options

MasonHub offers multiple integration methods to suit your business needs:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Shopify Integration">
    ### Direct Shopify Connection

    Perfect for Shopify merchants looking for seamless order management.

    **Benefits:**
    - Automatic order sync
    - Real-time inventory updates
    - Native Shopify app integration

    **Setup Time:** 15-30 minutes

    [Learn more about Shopify integration →](/help-center/shopify-integration)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="EDI Integration">
    ### Electronic Data Interchange

    Ideal for enterprise retailers and wholesale operations.

    **Benefits:**
    - Automated order processing
    - Bulk order handling
    - Industry-standard compliance

    **Setup Time:** 1-2 weeks

    [Learn more about EDI integration →](/help-center/edi-integration)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="API Integration">
    ### RESTful API

    For custom integrations and advanced use cases.

    **Benefits:**
    - Complete control and flexibility
    - Custom workflow support
    - Real-time data access

    **Setup Time:** Varies by complexity

    [View API documentation →](/api-reference)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Order Types

Understanding the different order types will help you choose the right workflow:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wholesale Orders" icon="warehouse">
    Bulk orders shipped to retailers, distributors, or business partners.

    **Key Features:**
    - Bulk pricing support
    - Custom packing slips
    - EDI compliance options
    - Net terms payment support

    [Wholesale Order Guide →](https://support.masonhub.co/hc/en-us/articles/30492880653204-Getting-Started-Wholesale-Order-Management-with-MasonHub)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Dropship Orders" icon="parachute-box">
    Direct-to-consumer orders shipped on behalf of your brand.

    **Key Features:**
    - Branded packing slips
    - Individual shipping
    - Consumer-friendly packaging
    - Return management

    [Dropship Introduction →](https://support.masonhub.co/hc/en-us/articles/28710035170068-Introduction-to-Dropshipping-with-MasonHub)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Manual Orders" icon="hand-pointer">
    Orders created directly in the MasonHub portal.

    **Key Features:**
    - Quick order entry
    - Sample and replacement orders
    - Custom shipping options
    - Override capabilities

    [Create Manual Orders →](/help-center/manual-orders)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Essential Configuration

### 1. User Management

Add team members and configure permissions:

- **Admin Users**: Full system access
- **Warehouse Users**: Fulfillment operations
- **Customer Service**: Order management and tracking
- **Read-Only**: Reporting and monitoring

[Manage Users →](/help-center/user-management)

### 2. Notification Settings

Stay informed with email notifications for:

- Order confirmations
- Shipping updates
- Inventory alerts
- System announcements

[Configure Notifications →](/help-center/notifications)

### 3. SKU Setup

Properly configure your product catalog:

<Check>
  **Required SKU Information:**
  - SKU identifier
  - Product description
  - Weight and dimensions
  - Barcode/UPC
  - Harmonized System (HS) codes for international shipping
</Check>
