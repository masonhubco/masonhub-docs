This guide walks you through the complete account setup process for MasonHub, from initial configuration to advanced settings.

## Initial Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Account Creation">
    ### Register Your Account

    Contact the MasonHub team to create your account. You'll need to provide:
    - Company name and details
    - Primary contact information
    - Business type (B2B, B2C, or hybrid)
    - Expected order volume
  </Step>

  <Step title="Login Credentials">
    ### Access Your Account

    Once your account is created, you'll receive:
    - **Account name** (your unique identifier)
    - **Temporary password** (change on first login)
    - **Portal URL** for access

    <Warning>
      Change your temporary password immediately upon first login for security.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Company Profile">
    ### Configure Basic Information

    Complete your company profile with:
    - Business address
    - Tax identification
    - Billing information
    - Brand assets (logos, colors)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## User Management

### Adding Team Members

Control who has access to your MasonHub account and what they can do.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Add Users">
    **To add a new user:**

    1. Navigate to **Settings** → **User Management**
    2. Click **Add User**
    3. Enter user details:
       - Full name
       - Email address
       - Role assignment
       - Department (optional)
    4. Set initial permissions
    5. Send invitation

    <Note>
      New users will receive an email invitation with login instructions.
    </Note>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="User Roles">
    **Available roles and permissions:**

    | Role | Permissions |
    |------|------------|
    | **Admin** | Full system access, user management, billing |
    | **Manager** | Order management, reporting, inventory |
    | **Fulfillment** | Order processing, shipping, warehouse ops |
    | **Customer Service** | Order viewing, tracking, customer notes |
    | **Finance** | Billing, invoices, financial reports |
    | **Read-Only** | View access only, no modifications |
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Remove Users">
    **To remove or deactivate a user:**

    1. Go to **Settings** → **User Management**
    2. Find the user in the list
    3. Click the **Actions** menu
    4. Select **Deactivate** (preserves history) or **Delete** (permanent)

    <Warning>
      Deleting a user is permanent. Consider deactivating instead to maintain audit trails.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Security Settings

### Protect Your Account

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Password Policy">
    **Strong password requirements:**
    - Minimum 12 characters
    - Mix of upper/lowercase letters
    - Include numbers and symbols
    - Change every 90 days
    - No password reuse for 12 cycles
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Two-Factor Auth">
    **Enable 2FA for enhanced security:**

    1. Go to **Account** → **Security**
    2. Click **Enable 2FA**
    3. Choose method:
       - SMS verification
       - Authenticator app
       - Email backup codes
    4. Complete verification
    5. Save backup codes securely
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="IP Restrictions">
    **Limit access by IP address:**

    Restrict account access to specific IP addresses or ranges for added security.

    Contact support to configure IP allowlisting.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
