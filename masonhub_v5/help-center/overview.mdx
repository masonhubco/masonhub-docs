## Popular Topics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I log into MasonHub?" icon="key">
    To log into the MasonHub Order Management System, you'll need:
    - **Account**: Your Shopify slug/store name as it appears in your URL
    - **Email**: Your registered email address
    - **Password**: Your account password

    Visit the [MasonHub Login](https://support.masonhub.co/hc/en-us/articles/360054561712-MasonHub-Login) page for detailed instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the different order types?" icon="layers">
    MasonHub supports multiple order types:
    - **Wholesale Orders**: Bulk orders to retailers and distributors
    - **Dropship Orders**: Direct-to-consumer fulfillment
    - **EDI Orders**: Electronic Data Interchange for automated processing

    Each type has specific workflows and management options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I track my inbound shipments?" icon="truck">
    Monitor your inbound shipments and ASNs (Advanced Shipping Notices) through the MasonHub portal.
    Check the [Inbound Shipments Status](https://support.masonhub.co/hc/en-us/articles/360052021812-Check-the-Status-of-Inbound-Shipments-ASNs) guide for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
