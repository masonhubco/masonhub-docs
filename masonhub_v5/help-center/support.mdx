## Support Process

<Note>
  Understanding our support process helps set proper expectations and ensures efficient resolution of your issues.
</Note>

### Support Tiers

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Standard Support">
    **Included with all accounts:**
    - Email support during business hours
    - Access to knowledge base
    - Portal support widget
    - Response within 2-4 hours
    - Resolution within 1-2 business days
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Priority Support">
    **For enterprise accounts:**
    - Dedicated support team
    - Phone support available
    - Response within 1 hour
    - 24/7 emergency hotline
    - Dedicated Slack channel
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Managed Services">
    **White-glove support:**
    - Dedicated account manager
    - Proactive monitoring
    - Custom reporting
    - Strategic consultations
    - Quarterly business reviews
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Submitting a Support Request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather Information">
    Collect relevant details:
    - Order numbers
    - Error messages
    - Screenshots
    - Timeline of events
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Channel">
    Select the appropriate support channel:
    - **Urgent**: Use support widget
    - **Complex**: Email with attachments
    - **Strategic**: Contact account manager
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Provide clear, detailed information:
    - Specific issue description
    - Impact on operations
    - Steps to reproduce
    - Desired outcome
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track Resolution">
    Monitor your request:
    - Check email for updates
    - Respond to clarification requests
    - Test proposed solutions
    - Confirm resolution
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Escalation Process

### When to Escalate

<Warning>
  Only escalate issues that meet these criteria:
  - Business-critical impact
  - No response within SLA
  - Recurring unresolved problem
  - Strategic concerns
</Warning>

### Escalation Contacts

1. **Level 1**: Support Team (support@masonhub.co)
2. **Level 2**: Support Manager
3. **Level 3**: Account Manager
4. **Level 4**: Executive Team

## Service Level Agreements

### Response Times

| Priority | Initial Response | Resolution Target |
|----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Critical** | 1 hour | 4 hours |
| **High** | 2 hours | 1 business day |
| **Medium** | 4 hours | 2 business days |
| **Low** | 8 hours | 5 business days |

### Priority Definitions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Critical">
    **Business operations halted:**
    - Complete system outage
    - Cannot process any orders
    - Data loss or corruption
    - Security breach
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="High">
    **Major impact:**
    - Partial system outage
    - Cannot process specific order types
    - Integration failures
    - Billing errors
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Medium">
    **Moderate impact:**
    - Non-critical feature issues
    - Reporting problems
    - Minor integration bugs
    - User access issues
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Low">
    **Minimal impact:**
    - Feature requests
    - General questions
    - Training needs
    - Documentation updates
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
