## Core Flows

### ERP → MasonHub:

- **SKUs**
- **Inbound Shipments**  
- **Orders**

### MasonHub → ERP:

- **Inbound Shipment Receipts**
- **Order Fulfillments**
- **Inventory Snapshots**
