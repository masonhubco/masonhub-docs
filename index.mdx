## Core Integration Flows

### ERP → MasonHub
- **SKUs** - Product catalog synchronization
- **Inbound Shipments** - Inventory receiving workflows
- **Orders** - Order creation and management

### MasonHub → ERP
- **Inbound Shipment Receipts** - Inventory receipt confirmations
- **Order Fulfillments** - Shipping and tracking updates
- **Inventory Snapshots** - Real-time inventory levels
